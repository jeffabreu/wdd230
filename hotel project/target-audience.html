Target Audience

The target audience for the Star Chamber Commerce will be primarily those who want to access information about opportunities of business and job. Those who want to find sucess in life and want to know where to start the star chamber commerce will provide what you need in terms of assistence and options.

Scenario 1

How the users will have access updated information?

Our home page will be updated frequently, so the users can check it there, but also they can go into the contact section and find another ways to access that information.

Scenario 2

About the assistence, where can I find it?

You will just need to click on the tab bar called "Get assistance" in the website and chat with a specialized attendent to that.

Scenario 3

How can I be a member?

To be elective to be a member click on Join with Us bottom and fill the survey.

Scenario 4

Will the members have a area only for them?

Yes, they will and will be updated about events and more through the same section.

Scenario 5

How can I support the projects?

You can send ideas and be part as a member.
